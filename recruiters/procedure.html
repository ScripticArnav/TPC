Placement Procedure

  • The Placement Season begins in the month of July and goes up till June of the following year - From July ‘18 to June ‘19.
  • Invitations with the placement timeline and other relevant information are sent out to organizations by the Placement Office.
  • Post intimation by a company, a list of interested candidates satisfying the company's criteria is prepared.
  • Tentative date for the companies visit is worked out. After confirmation, the office arranges a Pre-Placement Talk.
  • The company conducts its selection and shortlists students for the interview. The results are usually announced on the same day.
  • Students who receive confirmation letters are reserved for the company and not allowed to sit for any other visiting company. Also once into the selection process, no student can drop out in the intermediate stage,
